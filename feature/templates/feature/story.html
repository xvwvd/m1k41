A Tale of Two

A Therapist and a Zombie

It all began a long time ago, it was a story unlike any other. With the arrival of the great plague, the village was in shambles. People falling sick, cost of bread increasing, the village economy crumbling, it was a true horror. Amongst all these horrors, a little magical girl was also severely affected by the loss of her most beloved ones. Months had passed by, she could not smile again. However, in that same village, lived a poor little boy who also had been suffering for quite a long time. Even though a hundred things were falling down, he could not bear to see the girl remain sad and in distress. Often he would sit down in his prayers and pray for her. All he wanted was to see her smile again, see her like the happy little dumpling she was. It took a long time, but his prayers must have been answered. Everything soon got more normalised. Unwise by nature, the boy was in love with her. Days, weeks and months would go by, he would keep it to himself, confused and lost what to do. To the best of his wisdom, he knew such a feeling is forbidden, its unwise, its almost a blasphemy. But if you keep something as complicated as love stored up inside, how long would you last? It was only a matter of time before he felt dreadly suffocated. As time passed by, the girl had grown fascination for a guy she used to know. With each day, she would feel her feelings grow stronger, but one day, unfortunate events had taken its toll. She was hurt by the person she took a liking for. As much as she was crazy for that person, she would also realize that it is not the best for her, it would never work out with her, and she wanted to forget him.

Stuck inbetween, it felt like a matter of life and death, countless sleepless nights and countless thoughts and emotions made a zombie out of a poor living boy. He couldn't be alive without saying it, and he knew he would be dead if he did. His state of life has now rendered him a half-dead man. Once you start to suffocate, you will do anything to get out of it. He knew he had to let it out, no matter what happens. One summer night, he let his secret out.
